Campus Life

School Gymnasium

Was established in 2002

The school gymnasium is a very fun place. My class goes there every friday to do a class. The place is also big. It has many basketball hoops to play on. and we can also play other games like Soccer or Kick-Ball. Usually we also have assembelies in there. Because the Gym connects the with the Cafeteria with a wall that can be moved. And that is the gymnasium.



School Library

Was built in 2002

The school library is very big. It holds many books from Dr.Suess to Harry Potter. We have classes in the library too. Usually in November and May, it holds Book Fairs. And in April, it holds authors in april. That is when an Author from around America comes to our school and gives a presentation.



School Playground

Was built recently

The school playground is one of the funnest places in school. There are many slides, a soccer field, a basketball court, and a lot more. Usually every couple of years, the school tries to add more equipmant to play on. All grades have a schedule to come onto the playground. And everybody gets to go onto the playground at least twice on a regular school day.
